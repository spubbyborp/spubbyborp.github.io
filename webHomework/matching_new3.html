In this game, you will need to match each picture with its perfect copy!

You can choose between matching 8, 10, or 12 pictures, and you'll be give 3, 5, or 8 seconds respectively to memorize them, and 120, 150, or 180 seconds respectively to match them!

If you have an incorrect match, you'll be timed out for 3 seconds, so be careful!
